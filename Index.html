Customer Order Process

  • Greet the customer politely
  • Ask for their milk tea choice
  • Ask what flavor they want
  • Choose the size (small, medium, large)
  • Sugar level (0%, 25%, 50%, 75%, 100%)
  • Ask if they want extra tapioca
  • Ask their name for the order
  • Confirm the order before preparation

Equipment and Ingredients for Making Milk Tea

Equipment

  1. Bar spoon (small)
  2. Pearl scooper
  3. Coffee spoon (big)
  4. Clear jigger (small)
  5. Cocktail shaker (350ml)

Ingredients

  1. Black tea
  2. Brown sugar
  3. Milk
  4. Tapioca balls
  5. Water
  6. Ice
